Why a lawyer for global mobility?

In a recent ruling on July 29, 2024 (C-623/22), the European Court of Justice emphasized that the confidentiality of the relationship between the lawyer (avocat, advocaat, rechtsanwalt, attorney) and their client enjoys a very specific protection, which is related to the lawyer's special position within the judiciary and the fundamental task assigned to them.
The confidentiality and professional secrecy upheld by a Member of the Bar are unique and not found in other client-service provider relationships, such as those with consultants.
At Xelex, we highly value professional secrecy and are committed to never outsourcing any work or portions of work to back offices.
