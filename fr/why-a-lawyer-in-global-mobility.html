Why a lawyer for global mobility?

In recent rulings from July 29, 2024 (C-623/22) and September 24, 2024 (C-432/23), the European Court of Justice emphasised once again that the confidentiality of the relationship between the lawyer (avocat, advocaat, rechtsanwalt, attorney) and their client enjoys a very specific protection, which is related to the lawyer's special position within the judiciary and the fundamental task assigned to them.

The confidentiality and professional secrecy upheld by a Member of the Bar are unique and not found in other client-service provider relationships, such as those with consultants.

As lawyers specialised in Global Mobility we are dealing with extremely sensitive and personal information about our clients on a daily basis, we highly value professional secrecy and are committed to never outsource any work or portions of work to back offices or non-lawyers.
