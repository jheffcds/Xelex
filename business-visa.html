Business Visa

At Xelex we can assist with our client's business visa application process in a clear and smooth way, whether inbound or outbound Switzerland. Our services include:


  1. Visa assessment
  2. Assistance with the necessary documents needed for the visa application
  3. Filing of the visa application
  4. Personal accompaniment to the relevant diplomatic post
  5. Visa pick up service (if allowed)

A business visa is crucial for anyone seeking to engage in business activities in a foreign country, as it provides the legal authorisation necessary to comply with local immigration laws. This type of visa not only facilitates attendance at conferences, meetings, and networking events, which are vital for cultivating professional relationships, but also offers the opportunity to conduct market research. By securing a business visa, individuals can explore market conditions, meet potential clients or partners, and gather the information needed for informed decision-making. Furthermore, it enables the establishment and maintenance of partnerships with local businesses and stakeholders, which are essential for successful operations abroad. Additionally, holding a valid business visa enhances credibility and trust with local partners, clients, and authorities, demonstrating a serious commitment to doing business within their jurisdiction. The risks associated with travelling for business without the appropriate visa— including legal complications, fines, or deportation—are significantly reduced with a business visa. Typically allowing for longer stays than tourist visas, business visas provide ample time for conducting various business activities. In summary, obtaining a business visa is fundamental for those wishing to operate efficiently and effectively in a foreign market.
