Dirk Nuyts is a Member of the Brussels Bar since 1998 and has previously headed the Benelux and Swiss office of a major Corporate Immigration Law Firm. From 2019 until 2023 Dirk was EMEIA Immigration Markets Leader of a Big Four firm and overseeing 97 teams in Europe, the Middle East, India and Africa. With more than 25 years of experience in global mobility, with a focus on EU, Belgian, Luxembourg and Swiss Law Dirk has advised many multinationals with all immigration, international employment and international social security matters. Dirk has also advised several governments of EU Member States on different aspects of immigration law, including implementation of the Schengen Agreement, permanent residence and status of digital nomads. Dirk is currently heading the Belgian and Swiss office of Xelex Law Firm and advises corporate clients and High Net Worth Individuals with all their immigration and global mobility matters. Dirk speaks English, Dutch, French, German and has basic knowledge of Spanish and Portuguese.
