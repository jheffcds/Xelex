Xelex

The name Xelex is derived from a blend of the Greek word “xenos” and the Latin word “lex.”
The Greek term “xenos,” typically translated as foreigner or stranger, carries several positive connotations as well. It can refer to an ally, a lifelong friend, a guest, or a host.
On the other hand, the Latin word “lex” is a significant term in Roman law, encompassing statutory provisions and a collection of legal regulations.
Given that Xelex Law Firm specializes in various aspects of global mobility, including immigration law, the name Xelex was deemed fitting for the firm.




About Xelex

Xelex is a law firm, specialised in all aspects of global mobility for corporations, expatriates and high net worth individuals. With offices in Belgium and Switzerland, we mainly operate for all global mobility cases inbound Belgium, Switzerland and Luxembourg. Thanks to more than 25 years of expereince we can offer high-quality and clear advice in the constantly changing world of immigration, international employment and international social security.
We stand for personal high-quality involvement from senior lawyers and with -as a general standard- a fast turnaround and responsiveness of 1 businessday we strive for the highest client satisfaction.
We can personally assist our clients in English, French, German and Dutch oral and written communication. We can also assist in writing our Spanish, Portuguese and Italian speaking clients. In the near future we will add Mandarin and Japanese to our working languages.




Our Vision

At Xelex , our vision is to be the premier legal partner for individuals and businesses navigating the complexities of global mobility. We strive to empower our clients through unparalleled expertise in immigration law, international employment law, and international social security law.
We are dedicated to delivering fast turnaround times, ensuring that our clients can swiftly transition across borders without delay. Our commitment to integrity means that our clients can trust us to provide honest, transparent advice tailored to their unique situations.
As proactive problem-solvers, we anticipate challenges and seize opportunities, crafting innovative solutions that align with our clients' goals in an ever-changing global landscape. We aim to foster seamless mobility experiences where clients feel supported and informed every step of the way.
At Xelex, we envision a world where global mobility is not just a necessity but a catalyst for growth, collaboration, and success. Together, we will navigate the path to a more mobile future with confidence and clarity.
