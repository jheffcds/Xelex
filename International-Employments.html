International Employment

Globalization has redefined the way businesses operate, bringing new opportunities and challenges to international employment. Xelex offers comprehensive solutions that guide companies and individuals through the complexities of cross-border employment, ensuring compliance with local laws while maximizing operational efficiency. Whether you're a multinational corporation or an individual seeking international employment opportunities, our team of experts is here to assist with tailored services.


Employment Contracts
Crafting legally compliant and flexible employment contracts for cross-border hires is essential to ensure clarity and mutual agreement. Xelex specializes in developing employment contracts that account for the nuances of different jurisdictions. We ensure that contracts are both compliant with local labor laws and aligned with corporate goals, helping you build strong working relationships that endure.

International Social Security
Navigating social security systems in multiple countries can be challenging for both employers and employees. Xelex provides expert guidance on international social security agreements and benefits. Whether you're managing social security contributions for expatriates or looking for advice on totalization agreements, we help you ensure that workers are covered no matter where they are employed.
Posted Workers Notification
For businesses sending employees abroad for temporary assignments, compliance with the Posted Workers Directive is mandatory in many jurisdictions. Xelex assists companies with the proper notification and documentation processes, ensuring that posted workers are fully compliant with host country labor laws. Our service helps you avoid costly penalties while maintaining operational continuity.
Expat Policies
Having clear expat policies in place is key to managing a global workforce. Xelex helps businesses design and implement expat policies that cover all aspects of international assignments, from compensation packages to legal obligations. We make sure that both employers and employees are well-prepared, minimizing risk and ensuring a smooth transition for expatriates.
Tax
International employment often brings complex tax implications for both individuals and companies. Xelex provides comprehensive tax planning services, ensuring compliance with international tax laws and minimizing tax liabilities. We assist clients in managing their tax obligations across multiple jurisdictions, ensuring that they take full advantage of available tax benefits and avoid double taxation.
