LGBTQIA+

At Xelex we highly value the LGBTQIA+ community and make sure that their rights and protections are upheld. With our own lawyers or via our global legal network we can provide the following key legal services:


  1. LGTBTQIA+ policies: we can help draft a set of guidelines or principles adopted by our clients to promote equality and inclusion for their employees who identify as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, questioning, intersex or asexual.
  2. Family Law: Assistance with marriage, divorce, child custody, and adoption matters, particularly for same-sex couples or LGBTQ+ parents.
  3. Discrimination and Employment Law: Legal representation for cases of workplace discrimination based on sexual orientation or gender identity, and advice on filing complaints or lawsuits.
  4. Housing Law: Support in cases of discrimination in housing, including rental agreements and evictions.
  5. Healthcare Rights: Guidance on access to healthcare, including issues related to insurance, discrimination in medical settings, and transgender healthcare rights.
  6. Estate Planning: Help with wills, trusts, and powers of attorney, ensuring that partners' wishes are honored, especially in states where same-sex marriage laws may vary.
  7. Immigration Law: Assistance for LGBTQIA+ individuals facing persecution in their home countries or for same-sex couples navigating immigration processes.
  8. Transgender Rights: Legal support for name and gender marker changes, as well as representation in cases involving discrimination in various settings.
  9. Criminal Law: Defense against charges and support for individuals facing legal challenges related to their sexual orientation or gender identity.
  10. Public Accommodations: Legal assistance in cases of discrimination in public places, services, or businesses.
  11. Advocacy and Education: Providing information and resources on legal rights and protections for the LGBTQIA+ community, including outreach and training.
