High Net Worth Individuals

Switzerland is an excellent choice for High Net Worth Individuals (HNWI), and at Xelex, we offer support in obtaining residency permits and navigating the legal requirements for living and working in Switzerland.
We also assist with the lump sum taxation system available in several Swiss cantons specifically designed for these individuals.
In addition, our extensive legal network enables us to provide HNWIs with a variety of specialized legal services tailored to their unique needs and concerns, including Wealth Management and Estate Planning, Tax Advisory Services, Real Estate Transactions, Private Banking and Investment Services, Family Law, Corporate Law and Business Structuring, as well as Philanthropy and Charitable Giving.
